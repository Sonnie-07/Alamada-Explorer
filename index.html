Top Tourist Destinations in Alamada

Palepaz Falls

Palepaz Falls, also known as Pidatan Falls, is a majestic horsetail-type waterfall located in Sitio Patag, New Leon, Dado, Alamada. It’s known for its serene beauty and powerful cascades.

Asik-Asik Falls

Asik-Asik Falls, located in Barangay Upper Dado, is one of the most unique waterfalls in the Philippines, with curtain-like water flowing from lush green walls.

The Tent City

The Tent City offers colorful gardens and panoramic mountain views, making it a perfect blend of natural and man-made beauty, ideal for photography and relaxation.

Salukot Mountain View Resort

Salukot Mountain View Resort is a scenic resort surrounded by mountain landscapes and gardens perfect for unwinding and appreciating nature’s calmness.

Daday Falls

Hidden deep in Alamada’s forests, Daday Falls offers tranquility, clear waters, and a lush natural escape for adventure seekers.

Coco's Funville & Nature Park

Coco's Funville & Nature Park is a barn-themed attraction that combines countryside charm with recreation, giving visitors a Netherlands-inspired experience.

Cuartel De Campu Diez

Cuartel De Campu Diez is a charming, rustic cafe and homestay location in Rangayen, offering food and beverages, a themed garden, mountain views, and a cozy guest house.

Camp Heaven's View

Camp Heaven's View is a charming, rustic cafe and homestay location in Rangayen, perfect for a quiet escape and enjoying scenic garden views.

Pobre Hidden Spring Resort

Pobre Hidden Spring Resort is a serene getaway nestled in the lush landscapes of Alamada. It offers a tranquil environment, perfect for relaxation and reconnecting with nature.

J&A Highlands

J&A Highlands, located in Barangay Rangayen, Alamada, Cotabato, is a popular farm destination known for its scenic mountain views, cool climate, and relaxing atmosphere. It is also recognized as a Learning Site for Agriculture by the Department of Agriculture.

Raradangan River

Raradangan River is known for its clear flowing waters, lush surrounding greenery, and tranquil riverbanks, making it a peaceful and scenic spot for visitors and nature lovers.

Guo Peaks

Guo Peak likely refers to the Guo Hill View Deck in Alamada, Cotabato, which is a viewpoint offering scenic landscapes and is located between the barangays of Rangayen and Guiling. This area is known for its natural attractions.

About This Website

This website showcases the most beautiful and culturally rich destinations in Alamada, Cotabato. It serves as a travel guide for tourists, adventurers, and nature lovers, featuring detailed information, visuals, and descriptions of waterfalls, mountain resorts, scenic viewpoints, and eco-tourism sites. Its goal is to promote local tourism by highlighting popular attractions such as Asik-Asik Falls, Palepaz Falls, Guo Peak, and J&A Highlands, inspiring visitors to discover Alamada’s natural beauty. The site also supports responsible and sustainable tourism by sharing the environmental and cultural value of each destination. Overall, it offers users an informative, engaging, and visually appealing way to learn about and explore the wonders of Alamada.
